Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

We are open from 8:30 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday to Saturday. We are closed on Sundays and on national holidays: 26th January, 1st May, 15th August, and 2nd October.

Do I need to bring my own bags?

Yes, we encourage customers to bring their own carry bags to help reduce plastic waste and support our eco-friendly initiatives.

Do you offer delivery services?

Currently, we do not offer delivery services. All plants need to be purchased and collected directly from our nursery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI payments, and major credit/debit cards. For larger purchases, we can also accept bank transfers with prior arrangement.

Do you provide gardening advice?

Yes, our knowledgeable staff is always happy to provide gardening advice and help you select the right plants for your space and needs.
