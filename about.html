Welcome to Our Green Oasis

Maharashtra Nature Park Nursery is a heaven for plant lovers and gardening enthusiasts. Located in the heart of Mumbai, we are dedicated to promoting biodiversity and environmental awareness through the cultivation and distribution of a wide variety of plants.

Our nursery provides quality plants at affordable prices to encourage gardening and green spaces in urban environments. With hundreds of species ranging from medicinal herbs to ornamental plants, flowering varieties to fruit trees, we have something for every gardener.

Whether you're an experienced gardener or just starting your green journey, our knowledgeable staff is here to help you select the perfect plants for your space and provide expert advice on plant care.

Our Mission

To create a greener, healthier urban environment by making plants accessible to everyone. We believe that connecting with nature is essential for well-being, and we're committed to helping people bring more greenery into their lives.

Through sustainable practices, education, and community engagement, we aim to foster a culture of environmental stewardship and appreciation for the natural world.
